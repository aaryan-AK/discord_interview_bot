Cultural Fit:
1.	Can you describe a situation where you had to adapt to a significant change at work? How did you handle it?
2.	Tell us about a time when you worked in a team with diverse perspectives. What was your approach to collaboration?
3.	How do you align your personal values with the company's mission and values?
4.	What motivates you to perform at your best, and how do you think our company culture can support that?
5.	Can you give an example of how you contributed to a positive work environment in your last role?


Explanations of Technical Concepts:
6.	Describe a complex data analysis concept to someone who has no background in the field.
7.	Have you ever had to simplify a technical solution to a non-technical audience? How did you ensure they understood?
8.	Tell us about a time when you had to learn a new piece of technology or analytical method to solve a problem. How did you approach it?


Prove Resume and Experience under Questioning:
9.	Discuss a challenging data analytics project you worked on. What was your role, and how did you contribute to its success?
10.	Share an experience where you analyzed data and found a significant problem. What impact did this have on your decision-making process?
11.	Describe a time when you had to make a quick decision based on incomplete data. What was the outcome?
12.	Give an example of how you prioritized your tasks when handling multiple data analytics projects.



General Behavioral Questions:
13.	Tell me about a goal you set and achieved. What steps did you take to reach it?
14.	What interests you most about working at this company?
15.	What attracts you to a career in Data Analytics?
16.	Why are you the best fit for this role? Can you name your strengths and weaknesses?
17.	Can you share an instance where you had to handle a conflict within your team? How did you resolve it?
18.	Describe a situation where you had to work under a deadline. How did you manage your time and resources?
19.	Tell us about a time when you had to go above and beyond your regular responsibilities to get the job done.
20.	Give an example of a time when you received criticism for your work. How did you respond to the feedback?
21.	Describe a project where you had to use both quantitative and qualitative data. How did you integrate both types of data, and what was the result?
